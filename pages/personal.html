New to HI? Here's how to set it up →
1 What is HI? ▾

HI (Human Intelligence App) is a private, local-first personal life OS built for you and only you.

  • Stores your identity, daily focus, goals, projects, social contacts, and AI thinking.
  • Runs entirely on your device — no cloud sync, no third-party tracking.
  • Your data lives in your browser's local storage — not on any server.
  • Your data, your control. You decide what gets published and what stays private.

Think of HI as a personal operating system for your life: one place to think, plan, record, and own everything that matters to you.

2 Configure Your Identity ▾

Your identity is the foundation of HI. Follow these steps to get started:

  • 1 Open "Create Identity" → fill in your Full Name, Username, Email, Phone Code, Mobile Number, Roles, Location, and Life Mission → click Save.
  • 2 Your HDI (Human Digital Identity) is automatically generated from a hash of your identity — it appears in the hero panel above as a short code.
  • 3 Add your values and trust principles using the Personal tab → Trust Principles section.
Open Identity Setup →
3 Set Up Your Device ▾
  • Install HI as a PWA: on mobile tap "Add to Home Screen"; on desktop look for the install icon in your browser's address bar.
  • Device fingerprint: your device gets a unique fingerprint automatically — visible in the Jarvis status panel.
  • Trusted access: log in once and your session persists for 24 hours without needing to re-authenticate.
  • Offline mode: all your HI data is stored in-browser and works fully without an internet connection.
Jarvis API must be running locally for AI features to work.
4 Social Rights & Content Claims ▾
  • Everything you create in HI — notes, goals, projects, contacts — belongs exclusively to you.
  • Content published on kingofyadav.in is claimed under your HDI (Human Digital Identity).
  • Your HDI acts as a digital signature on your published content, proving authorship.
  • Social memory: contacts and events you add are private — never shared without your explicit action.
  • Content claim process: open the License page — your HDI is your claim certificate.
View License & HDI →
5 License & Ownership ▾
  • The License defines your rights over all content created through this platform.
  • Personal data: exclusively yours — never monetised, never sold.
  • Published content: licensed under your HDI signature — readable at /pages/hi-license.html.
  • Third-party use: any use of your published content requires attribution to your HDI.
  • Revocation: you can revoke access to any content claim at any time via the License page.
