Who I Am

Born 25 December 1999 in Bhagalpur, Bihar. I work at the intersection of digital systems, business building, community leadership, and social impact. I do not come from a background of formal institutional power or inherited networks. What I have built has been built step by step — through learning, coordination, visible accountability, and long-term consistency.

My identity is not a title. It is a record. The work either supports it or it doesn't.

What I Am Building

A structured life — not a brand, not a persona. A life with strong local relationships, credible digital presence, practical business systems, youth participation, and community problem-solving that can work beyond one person.

The goal is not to be known everywhere. The goal is to be trusted where it matters — by the people closest to the work, by the community I am part of, and over time, by anyone who examines the record and finds it consistent.

My Journey

  • 1999 — Born in Bhagalpur, Bihar

    Born on 25 December 1999. Early years shaped by family values, local culture, and the discipline of a Bhagalpur upbringing — a city with deep historical roots and a strong community identity.

  • 2015–2019 — Academic Foundation

    Completed school and early higher education in Bhagalpur. Began developing awareness of real community problems, economic gaps, and the distance between what institutions say and what ground-level reality looks like.

  • 2019–2021 — Ground-Level Learning

    Left formal learning to understand people directly. Spent time in community coordination, observing how problems are actually solved (or not), building real relationships, and learning how trust is earned versus assumed.

  • 2021–2023 — Business Systems & Digital Work

    Began building digital systems — websites, business profiles, service platforms. Learned how planning, communication, delivery, and accountability connect to build credible, functional operations.

  • 2023–Present — Ventures & Social Initiatives

    Launched three active ventures: Royal Heritage Resort (hospitality), Jhon Aamit LLP (registered brokerage firm), and National Youth Force (national NGO focused on children, women, youth, and environment). Began structured public communication.

  • 2024–Present — kingofyadav.in Digital Platform

    Built and launched a structured digital identity platform covering personal, professional, and social dimensions — including the HI App (private Life OS), Jarvis AI assistant, and HDI identity system.
